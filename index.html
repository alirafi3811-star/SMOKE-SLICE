Our Story

A kitchen built on smoke, pressed patties & slow-earned flavour.

Smoke & Slice was born out of a simple obsession: burgers pressed to order, pizza stretched by hand, and pasta finished the way an Italian grandmother would insist on. Every patty is homemade, every sauce made in-house, and every pie fired fresh — nothing sits waiting under a heat lamp.

Tucked inside the Imanta Tirgus Food Court in Rīga, we keep the kitchen open from morning past midnight, so a craving at noon or 1am gets the same care. Smoky, generous, unmistakably ours.
